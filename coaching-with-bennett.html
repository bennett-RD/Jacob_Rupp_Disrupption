About Bennett

Your Partner in
Unlocking Greatness

Bennett Schwartz is a High Performance Executive Coach who has spent years working with driven professionals, entrepreneurs, and executives who know they're capable of more — and are ready to prove it. His philosophy is simple: your results are a direct reflection of your beliefs, habits, and decisions. Change those, and everything changes.

Bennett's approach combines strategic thinking with deep inner work. He doesn't just help you set goals — he helps you understand exactly what's been keeping you from reaching them, and builds a clear, actionable path to break through. Every session is direct, focused, and designed to produce real shifts — not just good conversations.

As the author of Lost Then Found, Bennett draws on his own journey of transformation to meet clients exactly where they are. Whether you're navigating a career pivot, scaling a business, or simply ready to stop settling — Bennett brings the clarity, strategy, and accountability you need to live the life you truly desire.

Signature Framework

The 3 C's Formula

The framework behind every transformation Bennett leads.

After years of coaching high-performers across industries, Bennett identified three non-negotiables that separate those who achieve lasting change from those who stay stuck. This isn't theory — it's the exact framework applied in every coaching engagement.

C
Pillar 01

Clarity

You can't hit a target you can't see. Clarity is the foundation — getting crystal clear on who you are, what you want, and exactly what has been standing in the way.

C
Pillar 02

Commitment

Real change requires a real decision. Commitment means burning the boats — fully investing in the outcome with no back door, no excuses, and no looking back.

C
Pillar 03

Consistency

Transformation isn't a moment — it's a practice. Consistency is where the work becomes who you are, compounding results week after week, month after month.
