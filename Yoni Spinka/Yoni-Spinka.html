About Yoni

"I use fun as a gateway drug to serious personal change."

I'm Yoni Spinka — executive performance coach, Rabbi, and keynote speaker with over a decade of experience helping high-achievers dissolve fear and step into their power. I'm the CEO of Spinka Group Coaching & Consulting, based in the Philadelphia suburbs, where I live with my wife and five children.

My path to coaching was anything but linear. I spent seven years as Rabbi at the University of Pennsylvania, where I taught ancient Jewish spiritual practices and mentored hundreds of students navigating the pressures of achievement, identity, and purpose. That work showed me something: the most driven people are often carrying the heaviest invisible burdens.

Today, I blend that deep wisdom tradition with modern psychology, Internal Family Systems (IFS), and practical coaching methodology to help executives, entrepreneurs, and married men build lives that actually feel as good as they look from the outside.

My clients have included hedge fund managers, top executives at firms like Deloitte and Binance, Forbes 30 Under 30 honorees, law firm owners, and entrepreneurs across finance, technology, and entertainment. What they all share: the hunger for something more — more clarity, more balance, more aliveness.

When I'm not coaching, you'll find me on the tennis court, on the ice rink, biking with my kids, or gleaning wisdom from the Talmud. I bring all of it — depth, practicality, and a healthy dose of humor — into every session.
